How We Work

A clear process that keeps projects secure, focused, and predictable.

  1. Step 1 – Reach Out

    Tell us about your goals and context.

  2. Step 2 – Discovery Call (60 mins)

    Deep-dive on needs, constraints, and success metrics. A small discovery fee applies and is fully credited if you proceed.

  3. Step 3 – Proposal & Engagement Plan

    Scope, timeline, pricing, risks & mitigations (incl. security).

  4. Step 4 – Kickoff

    Finalize SOW, deposit invoice, comms rhythm, and security baselines.

  5. Step 5 – Build & Iterate

    Short sprints with demos. Security baked in: OWASP coding, dep checks, CI/CD hardening.

  6. Step 6 – Delivery & Handover

    UAT, docs, training, and security review of access & data handling.

Step 7 – Ongoing Support

  • Maintenance — updates, fixes, optimizations, security patches.
  • Implementation — executing consulting outcomes or rolling out new tech with the right controls.
