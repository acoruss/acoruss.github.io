How We Work

A clear, secure process that keeps projects focused and predictable from start to finish.

1

Reach Out

Tell us about your goals and context.

2

Discovery Call

60-minute deep-dive on needs, constraints, and success metrics.

Small discovery fee of $44 applies, fully credited if you proceed.

3

Proposal & Plan

Scope, timeline, pricing, risks & mitigations including security considerations.

4

Kickoff

Finalize SOW, deposit invoice, communication rhythm, and security baselines.

5

Build & Iterate

Short sprints with demos. Security baked in: OWASP coding, dependency checks, CI/CD hardening.

6

Delivery & Handover

User Acceptance Testing, documentation, training, and security review of access & data handling.

Ongoing Support Available

Maintenance

Updates, fixes, optimizations, and security patches.

Implementation

Executing consulting outcomes and rolling out new technology with proper controls.
