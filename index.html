How We Work

A clear process that keeps projects secure, focused, and predictable.

  1. Reach Out — Tell us about your goals and context.
  2. Discovery Call (60 mins) — Deep‑dive on needs and success metrics. Small fee credited if you proceed.
  3. Proposal & Engagement Plan — Scope, timeline, pricing, and risks (incl. security).
  4. Kickoff — Sign SOW, deposit, comms rhythm, access & data handling baselines.
  5. Build & Iterate — Short sprints, demos, OWASP‑aligned coding, CI/CD checks.
  6. Delivery & Handover — UAT, docs, training, and security review.
  7. Ongoing Support — Maintenance and implementation with controls.
